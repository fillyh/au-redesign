About Us

In early 2006, Eric and Carolyn Plaza started Absolute Urethane with over 15 years of experience in the roofing industry. Since then, the professionals at Absolute Urethane have been providing customers with high quality roofing and painting services. Our employees are trained and certified. Our business is family owned and operated.

Mission Statement

Absolute Urethane, Inc. strives to provide our customers with quality service, workmanship, and materials at a competitive price. In doing so, we are confident our company will flourish through repeated business and customer referrals.
